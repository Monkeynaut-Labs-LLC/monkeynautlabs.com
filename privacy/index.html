Scope

This policy applies to the Monkeynaut Labs LLC public website and describes general company privacy practices. A specific app may have additional privacy disclosures when required.

Website data practices

This website has no account system, contact form, advertising network, analytics service, or tracking script. Monkeynaut Labs LLC does not use this website to place advertising or analytics cookies.

The company hosting this website may process standard technical request information, such as IP address, browser type, requested page, and request time, to deliver and secure the site. Those practices are governed by the hosting provider’s terms and privacy documentation.

App-specific disclosures

Monkeynaut Labs apps are currently in development. App-specific listings will describe whether an app collects personal information. A specific app may have an additional privacy policy or disclosure covering its features, data use, third-party services, retention, and user choices.

This general policy should not be read as a statement that every future app will have identical data practices.

Information you send

If you contact Monkeynaut Labs LLC by email, we may receive your email address and any information you choose to provide. Support requests may include an app name, device model, operating system version, and issue description.

We use that information to respond, investigate the request, maintain relevant business records, and protect our services.

Sharing

Monkeynaut Labs LLC does not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers when reasonably necessary to operate company systems or respond to a request, or when disclosure is required by law or needed to protect rights, safety, and security.

Retention

Contact and support information is retained only as long as reasonably needed for the request, legitimate business records, security, dispute handling, or legal obligations. App-specific retention practices will be described separately when applicable.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards appropriate to the information and services involved. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the website, apps, or legal requirements change. The “Last updated” date will show when this general policy was most recently revised.

Privacy contact

Once business email is active, the intended privacy contact is privacy@monkeynautlabs.com.

Until custom-domain email is finalized, use the temporary fallback address MonkeynautLabs@outlook.com.
